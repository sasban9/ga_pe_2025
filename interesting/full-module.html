Key Takeaways

  • AI reflects its data — audit inputs and outputs.
  • Bias can come from data, labels, design, or deployment.
  • Use the 5-step ethical checklist on every project.
  • Document AI use and keep humans in the loop.

5‑Step Ethical Checklist

  1. Fairness: Who benefits? Who is excluded?
  2. Transparency: Can we explain it simply?
  3. Privacy: Any sensitive data involved?
  4. Accuracy: How will we verify outputs?
  5. Impact: Worst-case harms and mitigations.

Workbook Exercises (printable)

  1. Bias hunt: find one biased AI example and write 200 words.
  2. Re-prompt: reframe a biased prompt and compare outputs.
  3. Responsible use statement: draft a short disclosure for one AI tool you use.
