Mind Vitality

Cognitive decline robs seniors of independence, causes social isolation, and increases caregiver burden. Mind Vitality delivers personalized brain workouts with interfaces specifically designed for older adults.

Detailed Report

The platform automatically adjusts difficulty based on performance, creating the perfect balance of challenge and achievement to keep seniors engaged. Caregivers receive detailed progress reports highlighting which cognitive functions are improving and which need additional attention.
