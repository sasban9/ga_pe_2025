Making Editorial Changes

Finally, as I said before, you can always ask ChatGPT to refine your article.
For this purpose, you can use the "Canvas" feature, which is able to suggest edits, adjust the length, change reading level, add final polish, and even add emojis.
To access it, you pick the "Canvas" mode before inputting your query, and then start using it by inserting the text that you need to edit and prompting the task.
Depending on the nature of the task you want ChatGPT to perform, you should put a respectable prompt.
Here are some tips:
Write "Suggest Edits" if you want AI to give you some ideas for refinement.
Write "Adjust the tone of the text for [audience]" if you want the text to suit the audience better.
Write "Change the reading level to kindergarten" if you are targeting this specific age-group.
