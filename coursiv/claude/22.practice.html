So, let's start by testing your understanding of system prompts and task framing first. For this purpose, imagine a marketing team wants Claude to help evaluate product launch strategies. Which system prompt would be most effective for establishing a balanced analytical framework? You are a strategic marketing consultant with expertise in product launches. Analyze proposals using both quantitative metrics (ROI, conversion rates) and qualitative factors (brand alignment, customer experience). Present balanced assessments with pros, cons, and implementation considerations. You are a creative marketing director. Generate exciting product launch ideas with innovative approaches that will stand out in the marketplace. You are a marketing consultant who specializes in maximizing ROI for product launches. Focus exclusively on quantitative metrics and profitability. Avoid discussing qualitative factors as they're too subjective. You are a marketing expert. Evaluate product launch strategies and provide the best recommendation. Correct answer This system prompt effectively establishes a balanced analytical framework by defining a specific role with relevant expertise, specifying both quantitative and qualitative evaluation criteria, and structuring the output format to include multiple perspectives (pros, cons, implementation). It creates clear parameters without being overly restrictive. Next, consider a data scientist that needs to present research findings to executives. Which output formatting prompt would best serve this purpose? Present the research findings with full statistical details, including p-values, confidence intervals, and regression analyses. Include extensive technical explanations of the methodology. Summarize the research findings in simple terms that anyone can understand. Avoid technical details and focus on making the information accessible. Present the research findings in this format: (1) Executive Summary: 3-5 bullet points highlighting key insights. (2) Data Visualization: Describe one chart that best illustrates the main trend. (3) Business Implications: 2-3 concise paragraphs explaining impact on strategy. (4) Technical Appendix: Brief explanation of methodology for those interested in details. Present the research findings in a persuasive manner that emphasizes positive outcomes and minimizes limitations or caveats. Correct answer This prompt effectively balances executive-level communication needs by structuring the output with a clear hierarchy: high-level insights first (executive summary), visual representation of key data, business relevance, and technical details last for those who need them. This tiered approach ensures accessibility for executives while maintaining analytical rigor.
