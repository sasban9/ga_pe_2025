What is Copyright? So, what exactly is copyright? Think of it as a powerful shield for creators! It's a form of protection that grants exclusive rights to those who craft original works, whether they're literary, dramatic, musical, or artistic. As a creator, copyright gives you control over how your work is used, shared, and modified.
