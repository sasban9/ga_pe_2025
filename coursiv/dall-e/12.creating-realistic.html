Starting Simple: Basic Image Generation Before creating hyper-realistic images, let's start with a basic prompt to help us appreciate the journey from simple to sophisticated results. DALL-E Generate an image of a smartphone on a table.
Adding Realism Now, let's see the difference with a much more sophisticated prompt. DALL-E Generate a close-up shot of a sleek smartphone on a marble countertop , with soft, diffused studio lighting. The image should resemble a professional product photograph.
Adding Details Let's enhance our previous product image for even more realism. DALL-E Modify the smartphone image to include a reflection of studio lights . In the background, include a slightly out-of-focus coffee cup .
Creating a Hyper-Realistic Image Let's craft a prompt to generate a hyper-realistic image that pushes beyond normal photorealism. DALL-E A hyper-realistic image of an old sailor with a weather-beaten skin . Focus on capturing extreme detail on the eyes, and add sea spray in the air.
Another Example Let's create one more prompt that works within DALL-E's strengths for realism. DALL-E A hyper-realistic image of a vintage camera in a dimly lit antique shop , focusing on intricate metalwork and dust particles in a beam of light.
