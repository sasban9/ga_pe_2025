What is Inpainting? Inpainting is a powerful technique that allows you to edit specific parts of an image while keeping the rest intact. It's like having a magic paintbrush that understands what you want to create. How Does Inpainting Differ from Traditional Editing? Traditional editing tools often require manual brushwork or cloning. Inpainting with DALL-E, however, uses AI to understand the context and regenerate content that blends naturally into your image. It's smart, seamless, and incredibly efficient.
