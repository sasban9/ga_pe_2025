Authors know audiobooks are hot, but hiring voice actors costs $5,000+ and takes months or worse you read it yourself... no way. This desktop app uses AI voices to convert any text into professional-sounding audiobooks in a few hours instead of weeks. The key difference is everything happens on your computer without internet, so your unpublished manuscript never gets uploaded to some company's servers where it could leak.
Authors just drag their Word document into the app, pick a voice style, and hit generate. A few hours later they have a complete audiobook ready to sell on Audible. (You could streamline this process too as an upsell. TBD)
Here's how I'd grow it:
1. Start with $199 document to audiobook: platform.
