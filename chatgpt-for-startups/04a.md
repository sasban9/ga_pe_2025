### 🟨 LESSON 1 — The 3 A’s of AI Marketing: Audience, Angle, Action (3 min)

**On Screen:** “The 3 A’s Framework — Audience | Angle | Action”

**Voiceover Script:**

“Every piece of marketing content needs three core ingredients — Audience, Angle, and Action.

Audience: who are you talking to.
Angle: what emotion or motivation are you tapping into.
Action: what do you want them to do next.

If one of these is missing, your marketing falls flat.

Let’s teach ChatGPT this framework.”

**ChatGPT Demo (on screen):**

> **Prompt:**
> “Act as a growth strategist. For my startup [describe briefly], identify 3 audience segments, 3 emotional angles, and 3 possible actions to drive conversions.”

**Voiceover Commentary:**

“You’ll notice ChatGPT instantly creates a matrix of audience types — for example, early adopters, cost-sensitive users, or eco-conscious customers — along with the emotion that drives them, and a matching call-to-action.

This table becomes your growth compass — you’ll use it in every campaign moving forward.”

---

### 🟩 LESSON 2 — Content Engine: From One Idea to 20 Assets (4 min)

**On Screen:** “AI Content Engine: Repurpose One Idea Everywhere”

**Voiceover Script:**

“One of ChatGPT’s superpowers is creative expansion. You can take one message and instantly turn it into 20 content assets — posts, blogs, videos, emails, captions — all aligned around one core theme.”

**ChatGPT Demo (on screen):**

> **Prompt:**
> “Turn this core message — *‘We make sustainable packaging easy for small businesses’* — into:
>
> * a 500-word blog outline,
> * a catchy Instagram caption,
> * a LinkedIn post,
> * and a 30-second video script for reels.”

**Voiceover Commentary:**

“ChatGPT adapts tone and structure for each platform automatically.
The blog version will sound thoughtful and detailed.
The Instagram caption will sound punchy.
The video script will be conversational and call-to-action oriented.”

**Teaching Tip:**
“Encourage your learners to test tone commands like: *‘Make it sound like Apple.’* or *‘Rewrite this as a story from the founder’s perspective.’* You’ll see ChatGPT morph into your brand voice.”

**Mini Exercise (1 min):**
“Now it’s your turn. Pick one marketing message from your own startup and ask ChatGPT to create 5 variations across different formats.”

---

### 🟨 LESSON 3 — Persona-Based Storytelling (3 min)

**On Screen:** “Speak to a Person, Not a Market”

**Voiceover Script:**

“Most marketers make a big mistake — they write to an audience, not to a person.

ChatGPT can role-play as your ideal customer and speak their language, capturing how they *think, talk, and share.*

Let’s see it in action.”

**ChatGPT Demo:**

> **Prompt:**
> “Act as a 21-year-old student trying to start a side hustle. Write an Instagram caption announcing our new logo design tool in a way that makes them tag a friend.”

**Voiceover Commentary:**
“Notice the tone — playful, relatable, full of slang or emojis.
Now, let’s shift the persona.”

> **Follow-up Prompt:**
> “Rewrite this for a 40-year-old small business owner who values professionalism and ROI.”

**Voiceover Commentary:**
“Instantly, the tone shifts to confidence and credibility. That’s persona-based storytelling in action.”

**Teaching Moment:**
“Try this before launching any campaign — feed your buyer persona into ChatGPT and make it your audience simulator.”

### 🟩 LESSON 4 — Campaign Automation & Optimization (3 min)

**On Screen:** “Automate Planning. Optimize Results.”

**Voiceover Script:**

“Once your content is ready, ChatGPT can help plan, schedule, and improve it using performance psychology.

It doesn’t just write — it *thinks strategically.*”

**ChatGPT Demo:**

> **Prompt:**
> “Generate a 7-day LinkedIn content calendar for a SaaS startup that drives demo signups. Include post type, topic, CTA, and emotional tone.”

**Voiceover Commentary:**
“ChatGPT creates a day-by-day schedule — thought leadership Monday, testimonial Wednesday, product tip Friday.

Now let’s take it a step further.”

> **Prompt:**
> “Analyze these 10 email subject lines and suggest 3 high-performing rewrites using curiosity and urgency triggers.”

**Voiceover Commentary:**
“It even applies copywriting psychology — curiosity gaps, FOMO, and power words — to maximize open rates.

At this point, ChatGPT isn’t just assisting. It’s optimizing your marketing funnel.”

**Mini Exercise:**
“Take three posts you already have. Ask ChatGPT to ‘analyze and improve these posts for engagement and clarity.’ You’ll get instant coaching.”
