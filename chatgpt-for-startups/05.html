MODULE 5

Sales, Support & HR: Automate Conversations

Goal: Use ChatGPT to supercharge your startup’s communication-heavy departments — sales, customer support, and HR — so your team can scale without burning out.

"Your Startup Talks All Day"

Every startup lives or dies by its conversations — sales calls, support replies, and internal HR messages.

But writing, replying, and organizing all those words takes hours every day.

ChatGPT can automate most of this — and not with robotic templates, but with personalized, context-aware language that feels human.


By the end of this module, you’ll have three AI copilots: one for sales, one for support, and one for hiring.

PART 1 — SALES

Turn ChatGPT into Your Best Closer
AI in Sales = Preparation + Personalization

Most founders struggle to scale sales because every conversation feels unique. But with the right prompting, ChatGPT can help you research leads, craft outreach, and handle objections.


Let’s start with a simple prompt. 
> Act as a B2B sales strategist. 
    I sell [product/service] to [target audience]. 
    Create a cold email with a friendly tone, under 120 words, 
    that opens with a pain point, introduces our value, 
    and ends with a question.
Check result → emphasize tone control, relevance, and readability.
Now, take it further. Instead of rewriting manually, we’ll train ChatGPT to adjust tone. 
> Rewrite this same email in three tones:
>
> 1. Confident & corporate
> 2. Warm & conversational
> 3. Funny & slightly cheeky
Display output → See how AI gives multiple styles instantly.

Simulate Objection Handling

The real magic is when you use ChatGPT as a customer simulator. 
> You are a skeptical customer considering [product]. 
    Ask me tough questions about pricing, credibility, 
    and timing so I can practice my pitch.

Role-play live: answer ChatGPT’s objections, then ask it to rate your responses and suggest improvements.

Mini Exercise

PART 2 — CUSTOMER SUPPORT

Faster Replies, Happier Customers

AI Support Assistant

Customer support is where most startups lose time.

ChatGPT can draft replies, categorize tickets, and even defuse emotional customers while keeping your brand voice intact.

> Act as a customer support rep for [company name]. 
Here’s a customer complaint: [paste].
> Draft a calm, empathetic, and solution-oriented 
reply following these rules:
>
> * Start with empathy
> * Offer 1 actionable fix
> * End with reassurance
Read output → note tone and politeness.
Now we’ll make ChatGPT remember your tone permanently. 
> Whenever you write replies for [company name], 
always sound friendly, patient, and proactive. 
Avoid jargon. Confirm the issue, thank them, and show care.

You’ve just created your AI support tone profile.

Macro Responses & Summaries

ChatGPT can also summarize long chat logs into key insights. 
> Summarize these 5 customer chats into top 3 pain points 
and suggest one FAQ article idea for each.

Mini Exercise

Collect 3 of your most recent support emails and use ChatGPT to find patterns in complaints.

PART 3 — Human Resources

Automate Hiring, Onboarding & Feedback

AI in HR = Fair, Fast, and Friendly

Now let’s move inside your startup. HR tasks — writing job descriptions, screening candidates, conducting interviews — are repetitive but crucial. ChatGPT can reduce HR friction while keeping communication human.

Demo 1 (Job Description): 
> Write a clear, engaging job description 
for a [role] at a [type of company]. 
Include role summary, 3 key responsibilities, 
3 skills, and 1 culture note.
Display result → short, well-structured.
Prompt Demo 2 (Interview Questions): 
  > Generate 5 interview questions 
that test for curiosity and problem-solving, 
not just technical skill, for a [role].
ChatGPT can even simulate an interview. Prompt Demo 3 
> You’re an interviewer for [role]. 
Ask me one question at a time and score my answers 
from 1 to 10 on clarity, confidence, and relevance.

Let ChatGPT start the mock interview.
Mini Exercise Try one interview simulation with ChatGPT playing the interviewer.
