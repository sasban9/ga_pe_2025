## 🎬 MODULE 7: Finance, Legal & Operations — Simplify the Back Office

**Duration:** ~15 minutes
**Goal:** Show founders and startup operators how ChatGPT can save hours in bookkeeping, compliance, documentation, and daily management — the “boring but vital” side of running a startup.
**Core Idea:** You don’t need a full back-office team yet; ChatGPT can act as your analyst, paralegal, and ops assistant rolled into one.

---

### 🎙️INTRO (1 min)

**Voiceover:**
“Every founder loves the creative and product side. But what kills momentum? Paperwork, finances, and the fine print. In this module, I’ll show you how ChatGPT can manage these back-office chores like a tireless assistant — freeing you to focus on growth.”

**On screen:**

* Title: *ChatGPT for Finance, Legal & Operations*
* Subtitle: *Automate the boring. Focus on building.*

---

### 💰 PART 1 — FINANCE MADE SIMPLE (5 min)

**Voiceover:**
“Let’s start with finance — because knowing your numbers keeps you alive. ChatGPT isn’t a substitute for an accountant, but it’s perfect for building clarity, speed, and structure.”

---

#### 🧾 Lesson 1: Automating Financial Reports

**Voiceover:**
“Imagine you have monthly expenses in a spreadsheet — marketing, salaries, tools. Instead of manually analyzing, you can ask ChatGPT to summarize patterns.”

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “Summarize this table of expenses into total spend per category, key cost drivers, and 3 recommendations to reduce costs by 10%. Output in markdown table.”

**Live demo:** ChatGPT turns your messy list into a clean, readable report.

**Voiceover:**
“This kind of analysis helps you act like a CFO without needing one.”

---

#### 🧮 Lesson 2: Building Simple Financial Forecasts

**Voiceover:**
“Forecasting feels intimidating, but it’s just pattern prediction. ChatGPT can help you build a starter model.”

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “Create a 12-month financial projection for a SaaS startup earning $5,000 MRR growing 10% monthly, with 30% expenses. Show revenue, cost, and net profit in table form.”

**Voiceover:**
“In seconds, you get a projection you can paste into Sheets or Excel. Now you can tweak numbers and scenario-test.”

---

#### 📊 Lesson 3: Investor-Ready Summaries

**Voiceover:**
“Before a pitch, founders waste hours writing one-pagers. Let ChatGPT do the heavy lifting.”

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “Summarize our latest financial report into 5 bullet points suitable for an investor update email. Keep tone factual but optimistic.”

**Voiceover:**
“It creates professional summaries with the right tone and clarity — especially useful for monthly investor updates.”

---

### ⚖️ PART 2 — LEGAL CLARITY WITHOUT THE JARGON (5 min)

**Voiceover:**
“Legal documents are like foreign languages — and founders drown in them. ChatGPT can’t replace lawyers, but it’s a brilliant translator, summarizer, and risk detector.”

---

#### 📜 Lesson 1: Contract Summaries

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “Summarize this employment contract into key rights, obligations, and any red flags for the employer. Keep it under 200 words.”

**Voiceover:**
“This works for NDAs, vendor contracts, or partnership agreements. You’ll instantly understand what you’re signing — before your lawyer reviews it.”

---

#### ⚠️ Lesson 2: Risk Detection

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “Highlight 5 potential legal or operational risks in this vendor agreement and suggest what clauses we should clarify or negotiate.”

**Voiceover:**
“Now ChatGPT is acting like a paralegal — spotting issues before they become expensive.”

---

#### 🧠 Lesson 3: Policy Drafting

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “Create a privacy policy for a small e-commerce startup selling handmade goods online. Keep language simple and GDPR-compliant.”

**Voiceover:**
“You get a solid first draft. Then your legal advisor just reviews it, instead of writing from scratch.”

---

### ⚙️ PART 3 — OPERATIONS & WORKFLOW MAGIC (4 min)

**Voiceover:**
“Finally, let’s talk operations — the hidden backbone of startups. Here, ChatGPT becomes your Chief of Staff.”

---

#### 🗂️ Lesson 1: SOP Creation

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “Create a Standard Operating Procedure for onboarding new freelancers to our content team. Include steps, tools, and expected timelines.”

**Voiceover:**
“ChatGPT instantly structures a clear, repeatable process you can share internally — consistency, solved.”

---

#### 🧭 Lesson 2: Task & Priority Planning

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “You are my operations assistant. I’ll share our current goals and tasks — organize them into weekly priorities with deadlines and accountability owners.”

**Voiceover:**
“This turns chaos into clarity. Many founders literally run their operations through a ChatGPT chat thread like this daily.”

---

#### 🤖 Lesson 3: Workflow Automation Suggestions

**Prompt (on screen):**

> “Given our startup’s stack — Notion, Slack, Google Drive, and Stripe — suggest 5 automations we can set up using Zapier or Make to save manual effort.”

**Voiceover:**
“ChatGPT understands your toolset and recommends automation recipes. It’s like having an AI ops consultant.”

---

### 🧩 WRAP-UP (1 min)

**Voiceover:**
“Let’s recap: ChatGPT helps you run lean and smart. It won’t replace your finance or legal advisors — but it gives you superpowers between meetings. In one afternoon, you can summarize contracts, model cash flow, and design SOPs. That’s what we call an AI-powered back office.”

**On screen:**

* *Finance:* Summarize → Forecast → Report
* *Legal:* Simplify → Spot Risk → Draft
* *Operations:* Systemize → Prioritize → Automate

---

### 🧠 MINI PROJECT: “The 3-Hour CFO”

**Voiceover:**
“Here’s your hands-on challenge. In 3 prompts, build your AI-powered back office.”

1️⃣ Upload or paste your last month’s expenses → Ask for cost breakdown and insights.
2️⃣ Paste one legal agreement → Ask for summary and risks.
3️⃣ Ask ChatGPT to design one workflow automation for your team.

**Voiceover:**
“By the end of this exercise, you’ll have your first AI CFO, Legal Assistant, and Chief of Staff — all inside ChatGPT.”
