### “Simplify the Back Office with ChatGPT”

### 🎬 INTRO (Slide 1: “From Chaos to Clarity: Automating the Back Office”)

**Instructor narration:**
Most founders love the product and customer side of business. But the *back office* — finance, legal, operations — that’s where things get messy fast.
In this module, you’ll learn how to turn ChatGPT into your financial analyst, compliance assistant, and process designer — all without hiring a full-time team.

---

### 🧩 SECTION 1 — AI for Finance

**Slide:** “Turning Numbers into Narratives”

**Narration:**
Let’s start with finance. ChatGPT doesn’t just calculate — it *explains* your numbers.
Whether it’s forecasting next month’s cash flow or simplifying your balance sheet for an investor call, it can make financial clarity fast.

**Demo prompt:**

> “You are a startup CFO. Our product is a subscription app priced at ₹499 per month. We currently have 1,200 paying users and expect 10% monthly growth.
> Build a 6-month revenue forecast with costs and a short summary of our breakeven point.”

**Show result:** ChatGPT outputs a clean, structured forecast with tables.
**Narration:**
And the best part? You can add:

> “Now convert this into a Google Sheet formula layout”
> and ChatGPT will output formulas ready to paste into your spreadsheet.

**Mini Exercise:**
Ask learners to plug in their own pricing, cost structure, and user count, and generate their own 6-month forecast.

---

### 💡 Teaching Tip (on-screen text):

*“Always feed real data to get realistic outputs — ChatGPT is only as smart as the inputs you give it.”*

---

### 🧾 SECTION 2 — Legal Simplified

**Slide:** “Read Contracts Like a Lawyer (Without the Law Degree)”

**Narration:**
Next, legal. Founders spend hours reading contracts, NDAs, and policy documents they barely understand.
ChatGPT can act as your *legal translator* — not to replace a lawyer, but to help you *understand faster*.

**Demo prompt:**

> “Summarize this contract in plain English. Highlight key risks, renewal clauses, and termination conditions.”

**Show:** Paste a sample paragraph from a SaaS agreement.
**ChatGPT Output Example:**

* Renewal auto-renews yearly unless canceled 30 days before end date.
* Termination allowed for breach or insolvency.
* Risk: penalty for early exit is one month’s fee.

**Narration:**
Simple, human-readable, and you can follow up with:

> “List 3 questions I should ask my lawyer about this clause.”

ChatGPT then gives you a checklist for your legal discussion.

**Mini Exercise:**
Upload or paste a short contract from your own business (maybe a freelancer agreement) and have ChatGPT summarize it in bullet form.

---

### ⚙️ SECTION 3 — Operations & SOP Automation

**Slide:** “Standardize Everything”

**Narration:**
Now, let’s bring order to the daily chaos — your operations.
Every startup runs on repeatable processes: onboarding, reporting, client handoff, content review.
ChatGPT can draft, refine, and standardize these into living SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures).

**Demo prompt:**

> “Create a simple SOP for onboarding a new marketing intern at a startup. Include steps, responsible persons, tools used, and estimated time for each stage.”

**ChatGPT Output Example:**

1. Send welcome email (HR – Day 0)
2. Add to Slack, Notion, and drive (Ops – Day 1)
3. Assign first content task (Manager – Day 2)
4. Weekly check-in setup (Team Lead – Ongoing)

**Narration:**
You can then refine it by adding:

> “Format this as a table for Notion.”

Boom — you’ve got a ready-to-copy workflow template.

**Exercise:**
Pick one repeated task in your startup (for example: customer onboarding, social post approvals, or internal reporting) and get ChatGPT to build a step-by-step SOP.

---

### 📊 SECTION 4 — Forecasts, Reports, and Dashboards

**Slide:** “Data Storytelling Made Easy”

**Narration:**
Another overlooked use case — ChatGPT as a reporting assistant.
It can analyze your numbers, summarize key takeaways, and even create presentation talking points.

**Prompt Example:**

> “Given these metrics: [paste numbers], summarize what changed from last month, identify any red flags, and suggest 3 possible causes.”

**Narration:**
Instant executive summary — no more late-night Excel panic.

### ⚠️ SECTION 5 — AI Responsibility & Accuracy

**Slide:** “Trust but Verify”

**Narration:**
Remember — ChatGPT is brilliant, but not infallible.
For anything legal, tax, or compliance-related, always have a human review before acting.
Treat ChatGPT as your **assistant**, not your **authority.**

**On-screen text:**
✅ Draft → 👁️ Review → ✍️ Approve
That’s the workflow every smart startup should follow.
