## MODULE 5 — “Sales, Support & HR: Automate Conversations”

**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Goal:** Use ChatGPT to supercharge your startup’s communication-heavy departments — sales, customer support, and HR — so your team can scale without burning out.
**Tone:** Practical, upbeat, and founder-friendly.

---

### INTRO (1 min)

**Slide 1: “Your Startup Talks All Day”**
**Instructor (spoken):**
Every startup lives or dies by its conversations — sales calls, support replies, and internal HR messages. But writing, replying, and organizing all those words takes hours every day.

ChatGPT can automate most of this — and not with robotic templates, but with personalized, context-aware language that feels human.

By the end of this module, you’ll have three AI copilots: one for sales, one for support, and one for hiring.

---

### PART 1 — SALES: Turn ChatGPT into Your Best Closer (5 min)

**Slide 2: “AI in Sales = Preparation + Personalization”**
**Instructor:**
Most founders struggle to scale sales because every conversation feels unique. But with the right prompting, ChatGPT can help you research leads, craft outreach, and handle objections.

Let’s start with a simple prompt.

**Live Demo Prompt:**

> “Act as a B2B sales strategist. I sell [product/service] to [target audience]. Create a cold email with a friendly tone, under 120 words, that opens with a pain point, introduces our value, and ends with a question.”

Show result → emphasize tone control, relevance, and readability.

**Instructor:**
Now, take it further. Instead of rewriting manually, we’ll train ChatGPT to adjust tone.

**Prompt:**

> “Rewrite this same email in three tones:
>
> 1. Confident & corporate
> 2. Warm & conversational
> 3. Funny & slightly cheeky”

Display output → students see how AI gives multiple styles instantly.

**Slide 3: “Simulate Objection Handling”**
**Instructor:**
The real magic is when you use ChatGPT as a *customer simulator.*

**Prompt:**

> “You are a skeptical customer considering [product]. Ask me tough questions about pricing, credibility, and timing so I can practice my pitch.”

Role-play live: answer ChatGPT’s objections, then ask it to rate your responses and suggest improvements.

**Instructor Tip:**
Pause and let students try their own objection simulation for their product.

**Mini Exercise:**
Students will refine one of their sales replies using ChatGPT’s critique loop.

---

### PART 2 — CUSTOMER SUPPORT: Faster Replies, Happier Customers (5 min)

**Slide 4: “AI Support Assistant”**
**Instructor:**
Customer support is where most startups lose time. ChatGPT can draft replies, categorize tickets, and even defuse emotional customers while keeping your brand voice intact.

**Prompt Demo:**

> “Act as a customer support rep for [company name]. Here’s a customer complaint: [paste].
> Draft a calm, empathetic, and solution-oriented reply following these rules:
>
> * Start with empathy
> * Offer 1 actionable fix
> * End with reassurance”

Read output → note tone and politeness.

**Instructor:**
Now we’ll make ChatGPT remember your tone permanently.

**Prompt:**

> “Whenever you write replies for [company name], always sound friendly, patient, and proactive. Avoid jargon. Confirm the issue, thank them, and show care.”

You’ve just created your **AI support tone profile.**

**Slide 5: “Macro Responses & Summaries”**
**Instructor:**
ChatGPT can also summarize long chat logs into key insights.

**Prompt:**

> “Summarize these 5 customer chats into top 3 pain points and suggest one FAQ article idea for each.”

**Mini Exercise:**
Have learners collect 3 of their recent support emails and use ChatGPT to find patterns in complaints.

---

### PART 3 — HR: Automate Hiring, Onboarding & Feedback (4 min)

**Slide 6: “AI in HR = Fair, Fast, and Friendly”**
**Instructor:**
Now let’s move inside your startup. HR tasks — writing job descriptions, screening candidates, conducting interviews — are repetitive but crucial.
ChatGPT can reduce HR friction while keeping communication human.

**Prompt Demo 1 (Job Description):**

> “Write a clear, engaging job description for a [role] at a [type of company]. Include role summary, 3 key responsibilities, 3 skills, and 1 culture note.”

Display result → short, well-structured.

**Prompt Demo 2 (Interview Questions):**

> “Generate 5 interview questions that test for curiosity and problem-solving, not just technical skill, for a [role].”

**Instructor:**
ChatGPT can even simulate an interview.

**Prompt Demo 3:**

> “You’re an interviewer for [role]. Ask me one question at a time and score my answers from 1 to 10 on clarity, confidence, and relevance.”

Let ChatGPT start the “mock interview.”

**Mini Exercise:**
Students try one interview simulation with ChatGPT playing the interviewer.

---

### WRAP-UP (1 min)

**Slide 7: “Your Conversational AI Stack”**
**Instructor:**
Let’s recap. With the right structure:

* ChatGPT becomes your **Sales Trainer** — helping you write, practice, and improve.
* It’s your **Support Agent** — empathetic and consistent.
* It’s your **HR Assistant** — organized and bias-aware.

Together, they create the foundation for a startup that communicates at scale — 24/7, without burnout.

**Slide 8: “Your Homework”**
Ask students to:

1. Build a “Sales Agent,” “Support Rep,” and “HR Partner” prompt in ChatGPT.
2. Save each as a pinned conversation and update them weekly.

**Final Line (Spoken):**
You don’t need three departments to sound professional anymore — you just need one well-trained AI.
