## MODULE 4 — Marketing & Growth: Turn AI into Your Creative Team

**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Goal:** Use ChatGPT to plan, create, and optimize marketing campaigns that attract customers while saving time and budget.
**Mindset:** Treat ChatGPT as your “Head of Growth.” It can brainstorm, write, edit, and analyze — you provide the direction.

---

### LESSON 1 — The 3 A’s of AI Marketing: Audience, Angle, Action (3 min)

**Explain:** Every piece of marketing content needs these three building blocks.

* **Audience:** Who are we speaking to?
* **Angle:** What emotion or desire are we tapping into?
* **Action:** What’s the single next step we want them to take?

**Prompt Demo:**

> “Act as a growth strategist. For my startup [describe briefly], identify 3 audience segments, 3 core emotional angles, and 3 actions we can ask them to take.”

ChatGPT returns a table you can reuse across emails, ads, and social posts.

---

### LESSON 2 — Content Engine: From One Idea to 20 Assets (4 min)

Show learners how to turn a single core idea into a full campaign set.

**Prompt Demo:**

> “Turn this core message — *‘We make sustainable packaging easy for small businesses’* — into:
> • a blog outline,
> • an Instagram caption,
> • a LinkedIn post,
> • and a 30-second video script.”

**Explain:** ChatGPT will format it by channel, tone, and length. Then you can tweak brand voice or upload to a content calendar tool.

**Exercise:** Choose one of your startup messages and have ChatGPT expand it into 5 formats.

---

### LESSON 3 — Persona-Based Storytelling (3 min)

**Explain:** Great marketing talks *to* a person, not *at* a market.
Use ChatGPT to write in your customer’s language.

**Prompt Demo:**

> “Act as [persona: college student starting a side hustle]. Write an Instagram caption announcing our new logo-design tool in a way that would make them tag a friend.”

**Result:** You get copy that speaks emotionally and colloquially.
Encourage learners to iterate: “Now rewrite it for a 40-year-old small-business owner.”

---

### LESSON 4 — Campaign Automation & Optimization (3 min)

**Explain:** Once content exists, ChatGPT helps schedule, test, and refine.
Integrate with Google Sheets, Notion, or Zapier for real-world automation.

**Prompt Demo:**

> “Generate a 7-day content calendar for our LinkedIn page with themes, post types, and call-to-actions that drive demo signups.”

**Follow-up Prompt:**

> “Analyze this list of 10 email subject lines and suggest 3 high-performing variations based on open-rate psychology.”

**Exercise:** Learners feed in 3 posts they already have and ask ChatGPT to suggest better hooks or CTAs.

---

### LESSON 5 — Quick Win Recap (2 min)

Summarize with an “AI Growth Stack”:

1. **Ideation:** Audience → Angle → Action
2. **Creation:** Multi-format prompt
3. **Optimization:** Calendar + A/B testing

Encourage learners to run this 3-step cycle weekly so their marketing never stalls.

---

### MINI PROJECT — “Your AI Marketing Manager” (3 min)

Ask ChatGPT to act as their full-time marketing manager.

**Prompt Template:**

> “From now on, you are my startup’s Marketing Manager. You know our audience ([describe]), product ([describe]), and budget ([budget range]).
> Each week, generate a 3-post plan, 1 email draft, and 1 ad concept. Include hashtags, CTAs, and tone suggestions.”

Have students copy this into ChatGPT and watch as a full week of marketing appears in seconds.
