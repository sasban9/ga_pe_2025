The Big Idea
Work Less.
Produce More.
Tim Ferriss published The 4-Hour Workweek in 2007. His core premise:

You don't need to work 80 hours a week to live rich. You need to eliminate, automate, and delegate ruthlessly.
Then vs Now
2007 vs 2025
Then — Virtual Assistants
Human VAs in Bangalore. Outsource email triage, scheduling, basic research. Slow feedback loops, prone to misunderstanding, limited hours.
Now — AI Agents
Available 24 / 7. Reads emails, runs research, books meetings, codes scripts, writes drafts — in seconds. Zero time-zone friction.

Ferriss's Framework — Reimagined
D · E · A · L
  • D
    Define — What outcome do you actually need? Set the goal for your agent.
  • E
    Eliminate — Kill tasks that don't move the needle. Don't automate junk.
  • A
    Automate — Hand everything repeatable to an agent pipeline.
  • L
    Liberate — Reclaim the hours. Spend them on strategy, creativity, life.
