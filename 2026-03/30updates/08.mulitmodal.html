Where It All Began

The Original Contract

String in
→
Model
→
String out

Early NLP systems from ELIZA to GPT-2 treated language as the only signal. Input was tokenised text. Output was tokenised text. Everything else was someone else's problem.

03
Era 1 • 1960s–2019

Pure Language Models Ruled the World

🔤
Statistical NLP
n-grams, HMMs, CRFs. Bags of words, TF-IDF. Clever heuristics for a narrow job.
🧠
Neural Language Models
Word2Vec, ELMo, BERT. Representations that captured context — but still text only.
✉️
Encoder–Decoder
Seq2seq for translation. In: sentence. Out: sentence. A complete loop in strings.
📜
GPT-1 & GPT-2
Autoregressive text generation. Fluent, surprising — but blind, deaf, and mute.
04
The Bottleneck

Language Alone is a Leaky Bucket

Humans experience the world through multiple channels simultaneously. Text-only AI forced everything through a single straw — images described in words, speech transcribed before processing, video reduced to captions.

Information was lost at every step.

05
The Unlock · 2017

"Attention Is All You Need"

The Transformer's attention mechanism turned out to be modality-agnostic. It didn't care whether the sequence was words, pixels, or audio frames — only that there were tokens to relate.

— The architectural insight that made multimodal AI inevitable

Vaswani et al. aimed at translation. They accidentally handed the world a universal sequence reasoner.

09
The Gap

Pipelined vs. Native Multimodal

Pipelined
Speech → Transcription → LLM → TTS → Speech

Each step loses context. Latency stacks. Errors compound. The model never "hears" — it reads.
vs.
Native
Audio tokens in → unified model → audio tokens out

Tone, hesitation, emotion preserved. Real-time. One latency budget.
10
Era 3 · Hearing the World

Audio as a First-Class Modality

Before: Discrete pipeline
Whisper → GPT-4 → Eleven Labs
Separate models. Latency: 2–4 seconds. No prosody.
After: Native audio tokens
EnCodec / SoundStream discretise waveforms. Model attends to audio tokens just like text. Output is decoded directly to audio.

GPT-4o (2024) was the landmark demo: the model detects sarcasm in a voice, sings on request, and responds in under 300 ms.

11
The Core Insight

Everything Is Tokens

Word tokens Image patches Audio frames Video frames Code symbols Tool call JSON

Once you discretise a signal into tokens, the Transformer's attention doesn't distinguish modalities. The architecture generalises for free.

The challenge moved from "can we do it" to "how do we train it well."

12
The Hard Part

Training Multimodal at Scale

📦
Data Alignment
Paired image-text, audio-transcript, and video-caption datasets at the billion-example scale.
⚖️
Modality Balance
Text data vastly outnumbers audio data. Models underfit audio without careful curriculum scheduling.
💻
Context Length
A 60-second audio clip = thousands of tokens. Attention is quadratic. Long context is expensive.

14
Native Audio Streaming

Speaking Before Thinking Is Done

A truly native audio model generates speech tokens in parallel with reasoning tokens. The first syllable plays while the next sentence is still being planned.

This requires interleaved audio and text generation — a fundamentally different decoding strategy from sequential pipelines.

15
The Watershed · May 2024

GPT-4o: The Proof of Concept

🎙️
Sub-300ms voice
Human-level response latency in voice conversation for the first time.
😂
Paralinguistic cues
Detected laughter, sarcasm, and excitement in voice — not just words.
🔀
Unified model
One model end-to-end. No separate ASR, LLM, TTS stage. True native multimodal.
16
The Visual Frontier

Video: The 4th Dimension

Video adds time. Gemini 1.5 Pro processed up to 1 hour of video in a single context window — understanding narrative, tracking objects, and answering questions about events at specific timestamps.

This demands sparse frame sampling, efficient encoding, and temporal attention — new challenges beyond static image understanding.

Temporal reasoning Object tracking Scene understanding
17
Live Vision

Seeing the World in Real Time

Live Camera Feed
Stream your screen or phone camera. The model narrates, assists, and warns — like a co-pilot watching alongside you.
Bidirectional Vision
Not just describing what it sees — explaining code on a screen, reading handwritten notes, identifying objects you hold up.

Project Astra (Google DeepMind, 2024) demonstrated a universal AI agent that could remember where you left your glasses and help you with a physics problem written on a whiteboard.

18
Under the Hood

Architecture Evolution

Stage 1
Adapters
Frozen vision encoder + linear projection into LLM. Fast to train. Limited integration depth.
Stage 2
Cross-Attention
Language layers attend over visual features. Richer interaction but double the complexity.
Stage 3
Fully Unified
Single token stream. All modalities share the same self-attention across every layer. Native.
19
The Experience Layer

Latency Is the Product

< 300ms

The threshold below which a voice interaction feels like a conversation rather than a query-response loop. Human speech reaction time is 200–300ms.

Streaming multimodal models are the first AI systems to operate within this window — making AI feel present, not processed.
