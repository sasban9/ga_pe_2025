What Is a Reasoning Token?

A reasoning token is a unit of structured cognitive work performed by an AI model — the internal "thinking steps" consumed before producing an answer. Unlike output tokens (words you read), reasoning tokens are the invisible labor of inference: planning, evaluating, backtracking, concluding.

Economics of Scarcity
Scarce by Design

Reasoning tokens are computationally expensive. Each step of chain-of-thought consumes GPU cycles, memory bandwidth, and cooling energy. Unlike printing money, you cannot conjure reasoning tokens without real physical infrastructure — data centers, chips, power grids.

This physical grounding gives reasoning tokens their economic weight.
