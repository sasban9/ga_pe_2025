02 / 06 — The Job Market
Career Shift

"Prompt Engineer"
Is Fading Out

−40%

Job postings titled "Prompt Engineer" dropped ~40% from 2024 to 2025 — not because the skill lost value, but because it got absorbed into broader roles like AI workflow designer and AI product manager. Prompt literacy is now expected across professions, just like web literacy was in the 2000s.

03 / 06 — New Paradigm
Workflows

From Single Prompts
to Pipelines

2024
One-shot prompting
Craft one well-worded question. Get an answer. Iterate manually. Each prompt is standalone and disposable.
2026
Orchestrated pipelines
Multi-step flows where one AI's output feeds the next. Prompts are templated, version-controlled, and tested like code.
04 / 06 — New Role
Context Architecture

Prompting →
Context Engineering

  • ◈Structuring AI context with dynamic data, history, tools, and preferences — not just a single instruction.
  • ◈AI co-pilots prompting itself — you provide an initial query, the system rephrases and expands it for better results.
  • ◈Multimodal inputs are now standard — images, audio, and video are part of how you prompt, not exceptions.
  • ◈Brevity beats verbosity — the best prompts are under 100 words. Every word should carry constraint or context.
