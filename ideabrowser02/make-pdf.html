AI-Powered Career Readiness Program

Program Overview

The "AI-Powered Career Readiness Program" is a hybrid, industry-relevant initiative tailored for MBA/BBA students. This dual-track program helps students:

Track 1: Placement Preparation

Use AI tools to enhance placement preparation (resume, interview, LinkedIn).

Track 2: Role-Based AI Applications

Learn role-based applications of AI to perform better from day one in real jobs (Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations, Sales, Product).

Students will gain exposure to real-world AI tools and workflows used by modern companies, helping them stand out in placement processes and contribute meaningfully in their early careers.

Course Modules by Duration

1. 4-Hour Orientation Module

  • Trends in MBA placements & AI in business
  • Hands-on: Resume rewriting using ChatGPT
  • LinkedIn optimization using AI
  • AI across business functions: mini use-case showcase

2. 1-Day Workshop

  • Deep dive into AI resume, interview, and LinkedIn toolkit
  • AI tools for role-specific tasks (Marketing, HR, Finance, etc.)
  • Mini group activity: Role-based AI problem solving
  • Personal roadmap to continue learning post workshop

3. 2-Day Program

  • Track 1 + Full domain-based labs (Marketing, Finance, HR, etc.)
  • Resume, interview and LinkedIn completion with AI
  • Group project simulation (e.g., AI-powered campaign, hiring plan)
  • Hands-on: Canva AI, Midjourney, Power BI, Notion, Zapier, etc.

4. 3-Day Advanced Workshop

  • All of the above + Interview simulations, portfolio building
  • Project showcase: Team presentation of AI-enhanced solution
  • Exposure to agentic AI trends, prompt engineering mastery
  • Evaluation rubrics and the college can provide the certificate from their side.

5. 1-Week Bootcamp

  • Includes: Resume, interview, LinkedIn, domain-specific labs
  • Projects, cross-functional exposure, tool mastery
  • Guest lectures from AI-enabled professionals
  • Capstone project presentation + alumni group

Who Should Attend

  • Final-year & Pre-final MBA/BBA students (Non-Tech backgrounds)
  • Those targeting careers in Marketing, Finance, HR, Operations, Sales, Product roles
  • Colleges aiming to improve placement outcomes & student confidence

Program Benefits for Colleges

  • Improves placement readiness & early job performance
  • Bridges Tier-2 campus-industry gap with relevant, applied AI skills
  • Offers practical, job-aligned exposure to tools already adopted in BFSI, FMCG, Tech, Startups
  • Students become brand ambassadors with digital portfolios
  • Affordable, scalable, and customizable to college needs

Why This Matters Now

AI is transforming entry-level jobs. Companies expect MBA/BBA hires to work with AI-enhanced workflows:

  • 68% of BFSI and 45% of FMCG firms in India now use AI tools in operations, marketing, hiring.
  • Freshers with AI skills save up to 30% time and deliver 20% better output (industry benchmarks).
  • Without this foundation, students will miss opportunities and lag behind urban peers.

Adding this program now empowers your students to thrive in a tech-first workplace and stand out from the crowd.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Is this program suitable for non-technical MBA/BBA students?

Yes, it is designed for non-tech students. All tools are no-code and focused on business workflows.

Q2. Will students get certifications?

The college can provide the certificate from their side.

Q3. Can the program be integrated into our semester?

Yes, it can be conducted as a value-added workshop or elective module.

Q4. What tools will students use?

ChatGPT, Canva AI, Midjourney, Power BI, Notion AI, Zapier, Resume AI tools, and role-specific platforms.

Q5. How many students per batch?

Ideally 30–50 students per cohort. Can be scaled with faculty co-delivery.

Q6. Will students be job-ready after this?

They’ll have a clear career direction, improved resumes, interview confidence, and practical skills relevant to real-world roles.
