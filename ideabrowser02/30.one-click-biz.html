Small service businesses are drowning in admin work they never signed up for. Plumbers spend evenings texting confirmations. Dentists wrestle with clunky scheduling tools. Salon owners manually send the same "how was your visit?" messages over and over.
Service Hero changes everything with automation templates designed specifically for each industry. You choose your business type, select the workflows you need (appointment reminders, follow-ups, inventory alerts), and connect your existing tools. No coding, no tech headaches, just automation that works immediately.
The platform includes industry-specific templates like "Plumber Emergency Response," "Dental Appointment Sequence," and "Salon Rebooking Campaigns" that deploy in minutes, not months. You cut setup time by 50% compared to generic automation tools. Every template is built with conversion psychology and service business logic already baked in.
Service Hero grows by targeting industry Facebook groups and partnering with existing scheduling software as an add-on. You charge $99-299/month depending on business size, with implementation packages for those who want help setting everything up. For local businesses tired of paying consultants or struggling with tech that wasn't built for them, Service Hero turns operational chaos into organized, automated revenue-generating systems without the learning curve.
