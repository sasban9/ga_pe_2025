Hourly workers are trapped in unpredictable schedules while businesses scramble to fill last-minute vacancies. FlexWage solves both problems by creating a marketplace where workers can pick up extra shifts on their terms and businesses can instantly fill staffing gaps.
When someone can't make their shift, they post it to the app. Available workers in the same role get notified and can claim the shift with one tap. Businesses approve with a glance, and payroll adjusts automatically. You charge $49 - $$199/month per business location plus a small transaction fee on each successful shift swap.
The platform creates a win-win: workers gain schedule control and extra income, while businesses reduce no-shows and overtime costs. Start with restaurants and retail chains where shift swapping is already common but chaotic. Expand to healthcare, hospitality, and manufacturing where staffing flexibility directly impacts the bottom line. For growth, target multi-location businesses with 50+ hourly employees who feel the pain most acutely.
FlexWage's real power comes from the data network. As more workers and businesses join, you create a labor intelligence layer that predicts staffing needs, suggests optimal schedules, and helps businesses adapt to demand fluctuations in real-time. What begins as simple shift swapping evolves into the operating system for flexible work, potentially generating $1M-$10M ARR as you become essential infrastructure for hourly workforce management.
