Course Title

Smart Online Living: Digital Habits That Protect Your Future

Audience

Professionals, students, and households who want to be “digitally smart” without needing deep tech knowledge.

Format

10 modules, each 30 - 45 minutes. Every module introduces a key principle, real-world examples of what goes wrong when ignored, and a simple habit/exercise to implement right away.
Module 1

The Digital Foundation

* Why digital clutter and poor habits cause hidden stress
* Core principle: “What you separate, you can protect.”
* Exercise: Create two new email IDs (critical use vs. everyday).
Module 2

Passwords Without Pain

* History of password breaches and why reusing is dangerous
* Password managers explained simply
* Exercise: Install one manager (Bitwarden, 1Password, etc.),
add 5 logins today.
Module 3

Money Trail Management

* Subscriptions and hidden auto-renewals as modern leaks
* Building a single “payment channel”
* Exercise: Audit one month of card/UPI statements,
list all active recurring charges.
Module 4

Backup Like a Pro

* Why drives fail and clouds lock you out
* Two-system rule: at least 1 online + 1 offline backup
* Exercise: Turn on Google/Apple photo backup AND copy your “Documents” to a pendrive.
Module 5

Scam Immunity Protocol

* How urgency, fear, and authority are used against you
* Golden pause rule: never act on the channel the request came from
* Exercise: Collect 3 scam messages you've received and practice “pause + verify” response.
### Module 6 — Privacy & Tracking Detox * How apps harvest data in the background * Identifying which permissions are essential and which aren't * Exercise: Review top 10 apps on your phone, cut 3 you don't need.
