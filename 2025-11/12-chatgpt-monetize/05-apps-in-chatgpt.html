Apps in ChatGPT — The 2025 Money Engine

What it is: Apps inside ChatGPT are mini-products that run within the chat interface. They behave like software: they have UI, can collect structured inputs, run logic, fetch data, transform files, and return formatted outputs. They’re built using the ChatGPT Apps SDK, which is far simpler than traditional full-stack development.

Why this matters for earning: For the first time, you can build and deploy apps inside a platform that already has hundreds of millions of users. This gives you instant distribution and removes the pain of UI design, hosting, backend engineering, and customer acquisition.

If you create a useful app, the platform itself becomes your marketplace.

This is the closest thing to “the early days of the iOS App Store” but inside ChatGPT.

How users are monetising ChatGPT Apps right now

There are three direct ways people are earning:

1. Paid Apps / Paid Upgrades You build a free version inside ChatGPT, then unlock premium functionality behind a subscription, usage-based pricing, or a one-time payment. Examples: premium resume analyzers, tax-filing assistants, legal-document checkers, marketing-funnel generators.

2. Lead-Generating Apps for Agencies You build a free app that solves a small but high-value problem. Inside the output, you embed a call-to-action for a bigger paid service. Examples: a social-media-calendar generator that sells a monthly content-creation package, or a brand audit tool that upsells a full rebrand service.

3. Productised Services You turn a repeatable expert workflow into an app that customers can pay for repeatedly. This is especially powerful for designers, consultants, lawyers, accountants, recruiters, educators, and analysts.

Why Apps > Custom GPTs for making money

Custom GPTs are useful, but Apps have three major advantages:

They allow richer input (forms, modules, file processing).

They support reusable workflows built into code logic.

They can be used by any ChatGPT user instantly, mass-scaling the audience.

Many creators treat Apps as micro-SaaS inside ChatGPT.

Examples of high-income Apps you can build (2025-relevant)

A complete marketing automation engine that generates landing pages, ads, emails, and drip sequences.

A knowledge-base migration tool that takes Google Docs/Notion/PDFs and outputs a structured AI-ready knowledge graph.

A tax-saving assistant for freelancers in specific countries.

A manufacturing SOP generator timed to industry regulations.

An HR candidate-screening app that creates competency matrices, interview questions, and evaluation summaries.

You can build each of these in hours—not weeks.

Key insight for users wanting to earn from Gen-AI in 2025

The money no longer comes from text prompts. It comes from products that run on top of generative AI, and the new ChatGPT Apps ecosystem removes the single biggest barrier: building the product.
